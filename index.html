Clinical Language Annotation, Modeling and Processing Toolkit

CLAMP is a comprehensive clinical Natural Language Processing software that enables recognition and automatic encoding of clinical information in narrative patient reports. In addition to running clinical concept extraction as well as annotation pipelines, the individual components of the system can also be used as independent modules. The system lends itself for diverse applications in a broad range of clinical domains.

About Us

The CLAMP is a natural language processing tool, based on several award-winning methods and applications developed in University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston, School of Biomedical Informatics.

CLAMP Features

  • High-performance NLP components
  • Annotation and corpora management
  • Knowledge sources and sample clinical text
  • Machine learning and hybrid approaches
  • Customized pipelines
  • Interoperability and Scalability
